Padded Graphical Drop Cap

Padded Aligned Graphical Drop Cap. Text is large, bold, and aligned at the baseline. Subsequent lines are not indented.

Padded Floating Graphical Drop Cap. The drop cap is floated to the left and then offset to the right using padding-left. It has a background image rendered in the padding area. Subsequent lines are indented for as long as the drop cap is on their left.
